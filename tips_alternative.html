Essential Security Tips

Securing the digital world through awareness, action, and responsibility.

🔐 Secure Your Accounts

Enable multi-factor authentication and avoid password reuse. Use a password manager and protect recovery info. Log out on public devices and don't trust links in emails. Your accounts are only as strong as your weakest one.

🦠 Avoid Malware

Install reliable antivirus and keep your system updated. Don't download cracked software or open suspicious email attachments. Use a firewall and watch for system slowdowns or unexpected pop-ups.

🌐 Protect Your Privacy

Browse with a VPN and stick to HTTPS websites. Adjust privacy settings on your browser and social media. Don't overshare online, and revoke unused app permissions regularly.

🎣 Be Phish-Aware

Never trust unsolicited messages asking for logins. Check the sender's email and avoid clicking unknown links. Don't fall for scare tactics like "Your account is compromised!". Phishing is a hacker's favorite attack vector.

🧯 Backup Regularly

Ransomware can lock you out completely. Have backups on a cloud drive and external disk. Test your backups to ensure they actually restore. Use encrypted storage for sensitive files - it's your last line of defense.

📱 Lock Down Devices

Use biometrics or strong passcodes. Disable auto-connect for Wi-Fi/Bluetooth. Install security updates promptly. Don't root or jailbreak unless you understand the risks. If stolen, ensure attackers find nothing usable.
